About Single-Use Plastics

Single-use plastics are items used only once before being thrown away.

  • Plastic bags
  • Straws
  • Food containers
  • Water bottles

They are cheap and convenient but cause serious environmental problems.

Impact on Solid Waste Management

Increased Waste

Large amount of plastic waste increases pressure on waste systems.

Low Recycling

Only a small percentage of plastic is recycled properly.

Landfill Issues

Plastics take hundreds of years to decompose.

Pollution

Causes soil, water, and air pollution.

Case Study: India

India generates millions of tonnes of plastic waste every year.

  • Ban on single-use plastics (2022)
  • Plastic forms a major part of dry waste
  • Challenges: awareness, enforcement, and recycling

Solutions

Government

Ban plastics, improve recycling, enforce laws.

Community

Promote awareness and waste segregation.

Individuals

Use cloth bags and avoid disposable plastics.
